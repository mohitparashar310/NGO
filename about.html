About Us

Who we are ?

We are the group of people who think about other people's good and are always eager to provide helping hands for those who are in need, and we believe that change in society can only be brought about by starting with ourselves.

What is our role?

We organize, promote, undertake, sponsor, and carry out all Programs for the benefit of naturally challenged people, schedule caste, schedule tribes, people under the poverty line, old, aged widows, and downtrodden, irrespective of caste, creed, sex and gender.

What is our approach?

We organize programs for the promotion of pre-primary education, run maintain a hospital and nursing home care centers, distribute food packets to street children, provide food grains to old-age homes and orphanages.
